Pineapple Banana Smoothie

By: Verity78

A gorgeous and indulgent smoothie, but totally healthy!

Ingredients

  • Ice Cubes
  • Fresh Pineapple - peeled, cored, and cubed
  • Banana
  • Pineapple of Apple Juice

Steps

  1. Place ice cubes, pineapple, banana, and pineapple juice into the bowl of a blender.
  2. Puree on high until smooth.
