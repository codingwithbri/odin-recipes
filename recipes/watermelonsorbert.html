Watermelon Sorbert

By: Cooking in FL

Description

If you love watermelon and love sorbet, you're bound to love this simple recipe!

Ingredients

  • White Sugar
  • Water
  • Lemon Juice
  • Seeded Watermelon

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, water, and lemon juice in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir until sugar is dissolved, about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and refrigerate until cooled, about 30 minutes.
  3. Blend watermelon in a blender or food processor until pureed.
  4. Stir pureed watermelon into sugar mixture.
  5. Transfer watermelon mixture to an ice cream maker and freeze according to manufacturer's instructions.
